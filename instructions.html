How To Play - Treasure Hunter

Arrr! It's a Pirates life for you! In this game you will play as an adventuring swashbuckler looking for glory and gems! You will go from island to island, following your treasure maps to explore different regions in your quest for hidden treasure.

Game Setup: Choose TWO of the four Treasure Maps to Begin

Exploring: On your Turn, explore regions on your treasure maps by clicking on your maps to place the current exploration tile

Explore One Tile Option: If you cannot or do not want to place the current exploration tile, you may instead choose to explore just one area

Completed Cards: If you complete a treasure map, you earn points for the value of the map (the color, and number, in the upper left corner of each card) and for bonuses

Bonuses: Some cards have bonuses in the upper right corner. These count for the number of points listed on OTHER cards you have completed according to their suit/color

End of a Round: End of a Round: after 7 of the 8 Exploration tiles have been placed, the round it over

End of the Game: After four rounds, the game is over.
